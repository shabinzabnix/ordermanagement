**original_problem_statement:**
The user wants to build a comprehensive Enterprise Pharmacy Inventory Intelligence Platform for a pharmacy retail network.

**Initial Requirements (Phase 1 - Core System):**
- **Product Master:** Manage products via Excel bulk upload, including supplier details, pricing, and categories.
- **Store Master:** Admin can create and manage store locations.
- **Stock Uploads:** Head Office and individual stores can upload stock levels via Excel, with batch and expiry tracking. A key requirement is handling unit conversions (e.g., tablets to strips).
- **Consolidated Stock Report:** A network-wide dashboard showing stock levels for each product across all locations.
- **Inter-Store Transfer System:** A workflow for stores to request and approve stock transfers between locations.
- **Purchase Request Validation:** A system where stores create purchase requests, and the system validates them against network-wide stock availability before suggesting transfers or approving purchases.
- **User Management:** Role-based access control for Admin, Head Office Staff, and Store Staff.

**Subsequent Feature Enhancements:**
- **Phase 2 (Advanced Features):** Inventory Aging Report, RC Customer Management, Audit Logs, and an Intelligent Inventory Engine for dead stock detection.
- **Central CRM System:** A full CRM module for managing customer profiles (based on mobile number), tracking medicine purchases from daily sales reports (via Excel upload), and managing refill reminders.
- **PO Manager:** A dedicated module for Head Office to create, manage, and approve Purchase Orders, including creating POs from sub-category-based Excel uploads and generating PDFs with a company letterhead.
- **Unified Intelligence Layer:** A central dashboard for high-level analytics on inventory, customer, and operational intelligence. This includes demand forecasting, expiry risk detection, and smart purchase recommendations.
- **Store Performance & Analytics:** Dashboards for store performance scorecards and comparing sales vs. purchase data.
- **Store Request Workflow:** A sophisticated workflow for store-level purchase requests, including multi-stage approvals (Pending -> Approved -> Order Placed -> Received), per-item status tracking, and an integrated chat system for communication.
- **Role & Access Control:** Added  and  roles, and a feature to assign specific service/module access to users upon creation.
- **Customer Profile Enhancements:** The ability to convert walk-in customers to RC (recurring) customers and track detailed medication schedules (dosage, timing, food relation).
